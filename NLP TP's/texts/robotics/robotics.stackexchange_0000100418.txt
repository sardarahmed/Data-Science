Q:

How to get nav2 BT state/running nodes

Is there any topic or service from where you can get the current state of the BT? How to know for example when the BT entered a recovery branch and its what iis the status of its childs?

Post score: 0

A:

Yup! Check out the nav2_msgs package! We publish some behavior tree status messages regularly. The topic is subject to change but if you search for these types you can find the specific topic used in your distribution.
https://github.com/ros-planning/navigation2/tree/main/nav2_msgs/msg
