Q:

what's audio type of audio_common msg

on android,I want to receive and play audio recorded on ros, but I don't know stream type of the audio, is it mp3 or PWM, and what's sample rate and bit rate, confused ?

A:

While the message itself doesn't have any requirements, if you want the audio_play or audio_record nodes to work, all data should be mp3 (and specifically 192kbit/s).
