A:

It looks like your ROS_PACKAGE_PATH is not defined. Be sure to follow the instructions for installing rosjava. And be sure to:
$ cd my_ros_workspace
$ source setup.bash

It's best to add the source setup.bash line to your bash.rc.
I've also filed an issue to provide a more informative error message.
