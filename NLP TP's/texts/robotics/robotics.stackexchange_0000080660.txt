Q:

navigation stack and static map

what is the importance of static map in navigation stack?? and what if it doesn't exist??

Originally posted by Moon on ROS Answers with karma: 19 on 2017-05-06
Post score: 0

A:

The static map is e.g. the floor plan of your building and used for global navigation. (How to drive from room A to room B). You don't have to use it, but without it your robot would not know which path to take from A to B.
