A:

For this kind of requirements we provide Orocos. We provide a integration with ROS in our repository allowing to do your low level real-time control with Orocos and still make use of high level algorithms like the navigation stack from ROS.
This kind of setup was already demonstrated on multiple robots like the RoboEarth Amigo platform, the Kuka Youbot, ...
