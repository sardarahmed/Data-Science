I want to launch rxbag from a launch file and load the appropiate data directly.
is that possible? I've been looking but I found no examples or anything about launching rxbag from a launchfile.

A:

<node pkg="rosbag" type="play" name="play" args="--bag /path/to/your/filename.bag --rate 0.5"  />
