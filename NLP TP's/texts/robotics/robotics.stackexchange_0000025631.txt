Q:

Gazebo and Ogre meshes

Why Gazebo lost support of opening ogre meshes directly?

Originally posted by rlklaser on Gazebo Answers with karma: 1 on 2013-01-16
Post score: 0

A:

Ogre meshes are not very common, and are not supported by most other applications. Rather than try to support a large range of formats, we have decided to limit ourselves to the most common and/or most universally accepted (collada and stl).
