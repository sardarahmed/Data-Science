What are you using for frame in RVIZ?

A:

add
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<robot name="dyn_controller" xmlns:xacro="http://www.ros.org/wiki/xacro">
  <joint name="imu_joint" type="fixed">
    <parent link="base_link" />
    <child link="imu_link" />
  </joint>
</robot>
