A:

This looks like you're hitting a bug in the compiler:
c++: internal compiler error: Killed (program cc1plus)

It sounds like you should ask about this on the openrave mailing list, along with the relevant information about your system, such as OS version, ROS version, and the version of the openrave stack that you're trying to compile.
As a side note, the openrave_planning stack hasn't been released since Fuerte, so I suspect is isn't very well supported in later versions of ROS.
