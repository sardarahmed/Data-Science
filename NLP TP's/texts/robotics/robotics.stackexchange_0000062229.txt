Q:

Can I use gazebo 3.0 with ros-hydro on Ubuntu Trusty 14.04

I have red Gazebo is a standalone application. Does Hydro support Gazebo 3.0 ?

Originally posted by tonyParker on ROS Answers with karma: 377 on 2014-08-30

A:

No, this is not supported in the official builds of ROS.
ROS Hydro supports Gazebo 1.9 on Ubuntu 12.04, 12.10 and 13.04
ROS Indigo supports Gazebo 2.0 on Ubuntu 13.10 and 14.04
