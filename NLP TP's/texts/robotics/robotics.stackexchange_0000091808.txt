A:

roslaunch robot1_description display.launch model:="rospack find robot1_description/urdf/robot1.urdf"

You need to use backticks, not regular quotes. This is bash, not roslaunch.
The correct command would be:
roslaunch robot1_description display.launch model:="`rospack find robot1_description`/urdf/robot1.urdf"
