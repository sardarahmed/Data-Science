Q:

how to ping the IP_OF_WORKSTATION

what is the procedure of how to  ping this when i am using the turtle bot system and the OS is the Ubuntu 12.04 version
it prompt me for "unknown host IP_OF_WORKSTATION"

Post score: 0

A:

You need to replace IP_OF_WORKSTATION with the IP address of your workstation.
For example, if the IP address of your workstation is 192.168.42.42, you would do:
ping 192.168.42.42
