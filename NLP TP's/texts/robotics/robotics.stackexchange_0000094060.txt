Q:

How do I implement other path planing algrithm for ROS global_planner?

I want to implement other path planning algrithm for ROS robot navigation,but I don't how to do it.

A:

Hi! Basically, what you have to do is to implement planner that adheres to the nav_core::BaseGlobalPlanner C++ interface in nav_core package.
Follow this tutorial, it quite nicely explains procedure of making your global planner as a plug-in to Navigation Stack:
http://wiki.ros.org/navigation/Tutorials/Writing%20A%20Global%20Path%20Planner%20As%20Plugin%20in%20ROS
