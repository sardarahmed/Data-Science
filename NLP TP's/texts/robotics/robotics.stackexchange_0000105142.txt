A:

Alright, to clarify: the problem occurs only when you execute the command rosrun map_server map_server mylimomap.yaml, and not from the launch file, correct?
Try this checklist:

Source your ROS distribution.
Confirm that both .yaml and .pgm files reside in the same directory.
Verify the filename specified inside the .yaml matches the .pgm filename.
Execute:rosrun map_server map_server path_to_your_map_folder/mylimomap.yaml
Make sure the topic for the map is set to /map
