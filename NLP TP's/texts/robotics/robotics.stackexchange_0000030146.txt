Q:

Unpairing a PR2 from a basestation

How do you unpair a PR2 from a basestation?

Originally posted by edgan on ROS Answers with karma: 66 on 2011-03-07
Post score: 0

A:

Edit /etc/robot-foward.conf on the base station, and remove the corresponding lines for the robot you want to unpair. Then run "sudo robot-forward start".
