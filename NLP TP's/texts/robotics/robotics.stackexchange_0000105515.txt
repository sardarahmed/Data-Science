A:

after i replace 1000 from 10000 everything was fine

Glad you figured it out, but you'd be better off using the static_transform_publisher from the tf2_ros package instead. With this newer improved node, you don't specify a period_in_millisec at all:
<node pkg="tf2_ros" type="static_transform_publisher" name="myname" args="x y z yaw pitch roll parent_frame child_frame" />
