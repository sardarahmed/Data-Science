Q:

confidence value skeleton_tracker

what does mean the confidence value of skeleton_tracker?

Originally posted by idavico on ROS Answers with karma: 18 on 2014-10-08
Post score: 0

A:

Each joint of the tracked skeleton has a confidence value that represents the goodness/reliability of its position. 1 means good tracking, while 0 bad or no tracking.
