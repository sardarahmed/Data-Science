A:

Maybe a little too late for an answer, but there is one for the sake of documentation. RTAB-Map works only with ROS Hydro and newer ROS versions. It uses catkin build system, which is maybe your problem on Fuerte. Visit https://github.com/introlab/rtabmap_ros for installation instructions.
