Q:

How to give wheel radius in ros_stage?

We want to design a robot in stage.
Here are the required specifications:
wheel radius=15 cm
distance between two wheels = 40 cm
how these can be added in the design?
Thanks!

A:

Stage does not simulate robot motion on this physical level. You probably want to use Gazebo instead, if you want to physically simulate your robot. In Stage a robot simply has a velocity and direction where it moves to following a given command.
