Q:

What’s the significance of the tags in ‘dtLane’

Hello ,everyone
What is the significance of ‘Dist’,‘PID’,‘Dir’, ‘Apara’,‘r’,‘slope’,‘cant’,‘LW’,‘RW’ in dtlane vector maps?

Originally posted by NJUST on ROS Answers with karma: 3 on 2020-08-06
Post score: 0

A:

each record represent a waypoint, with
Dist: never used
PID: ID of the point in point.csv
Dir : Orientation of the waypoint
Apara, r, cant: never used
LW: to point to the left waypoint (for lane change)
RW: to point to the right waypoint (for lane change)
