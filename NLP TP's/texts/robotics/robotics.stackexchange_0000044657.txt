A:

The name of the package includes the distro name.
So try:
 $ sudo aptitude remove ros-electric-ros

and/or
 $ sudo aptitude remove ros-fuerte-ros

You can see what is installed by running
$ aptitude search ros-

Those with a small i at the beginning of the line are installed.
