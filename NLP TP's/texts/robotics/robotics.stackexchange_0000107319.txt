A:

This was totally my mistake. I remapped the /cmd_vel topic from the move_base to the /diff_drive_controller/cmd_vel from the diff_drive_controller in the move_base.launch file like this
<node name="move_base" pkg="move_base" respawn="false" type="move_base" output="screen">
    <!-- Other parameters for move_base -->
    <remap from="/cmd_vel" to="/diff_drive_controller/cmd_vel"/>
</node>

now the robot moves like a charm. Thanks for your insight @Russ76
