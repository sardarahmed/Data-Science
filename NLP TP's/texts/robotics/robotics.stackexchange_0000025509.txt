A:

drc robot model in drcsim 1.2 refers to multisense_sl model meshes.  Check if your installed files includes /usr/share/drcim-1.2/models/multisense_sl/meshes/base_link.STL and /usr/share/drcim-1.2/models/multisense_sl/meshes/hokuyo_link.STL as shown in the bitbucket link.
