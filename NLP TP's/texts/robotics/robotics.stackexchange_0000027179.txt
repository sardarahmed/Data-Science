Q:

Create Vehicle with Suspension?

Is it possible to create a vehicle model in Gazebo (preferably versions 2 or 3) with a suspension system?

Originally posted by K. Zeng on Gazebo Answers with karma: 103 on 2014-10-31
Post score: 0

A:

The vehicle defined in the ackermann_vehicle metapackage (http://wiki.ros.org/ackermann_vehicle) has a shock absorber for each wheel.
