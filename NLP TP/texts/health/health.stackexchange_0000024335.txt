Q:

In the Moderna phase 3 clinical trial of the vaccine for COVID-19 what is in the placebo?

Moderna has started a phase 3 clinical trial to evaluate if their vaccine can prevent coronavirus disease 2019.
What specifically is in the placebo?

A:

According to an NIH press release the trial will use a "saline placebo", in two doses mirroring the two doses of active ingredient.
Saline is a fairly typical placebo.
