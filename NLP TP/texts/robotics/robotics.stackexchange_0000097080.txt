Q:

Change occupancy grid axis

Hi!
I have lots of information been published to the XZ axis. Now, I'm publishing a 2D occupancy grid, but it is in the XY axis.
It is possible to change it to XZ as well?
Thanks in advance.

Originally posted by mirella melo on ROS Answers with karma: 194 on 2020-09-13
Post score: 0

A:

grid_map.info.origin.orientation.x = 0.7071068;
grid_map.info.origin.orientation.y = 0.0;
grid_map.info.origin.orientation.z = 0.0;
grid_map.info.origin.orientation.w = 0.7071068;
