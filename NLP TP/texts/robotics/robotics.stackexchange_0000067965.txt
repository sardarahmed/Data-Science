A:

Hi,
This is more of a Gazebo question (http://answers.gazebosim.org/questions/) 
What you should do is open your world: 
 roslaunch gazebo_ros empty_world.launch world_name:=YOUR_WORLD_FILE
Then you modify it (add objects etc)
Finally save your world again.
Hope this helps
