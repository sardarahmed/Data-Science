A:

I suppose your model will be in .3ds format. I suggest you follow this procedure.

Convert to .dae or .stl using MeshLab (or any software). I prefer converting to .stl using MeshLab

In the  or  tag of your model file specify the size of the bounding box (or cylinder or sphere).

Specify the pose of bounding box in your world through  and 

If you are using .stl specify the material in  (beware of materials available on fuerte)
