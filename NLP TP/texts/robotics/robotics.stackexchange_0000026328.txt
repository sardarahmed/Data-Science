How to turn on disabled messages in drcsim nightly temporarily?

such as:
/atlas/debug/l_foot_contact

A:

The topics you mention are part of the development aids.  Starting in the forthcoming drcsim 2.6.0 (and in the current nightly builds), they are disabled by default.  To enable them, set the environment variable VRC_CHEATS_ENABLED to 1, as described in the User Guide.
