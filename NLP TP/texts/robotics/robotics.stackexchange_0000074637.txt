Q:

Why do we need mavros?

Can someone please explain in simple words, why do we need mavros ? I have been trying to understand mavros form the documentation but did not understand it completely.

A:

mavros makes it possible to communicate with a MavLink autopilot via ROS topics
It does this by publishing sensor data and status from the autopilot as ROS topics, and by subscribing to other ROS topics for commands, and then sending those commands to the autopilot.
