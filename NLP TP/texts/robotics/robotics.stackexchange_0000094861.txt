Q:

how can i add ultrasonic sensor to husky simulation in gazebo?

i want to permanently add sensors to husky and want to know the methodology generally for any sensor and if there special cases to deal with it?

A:

Just add it to your URDF (and SDF if appropriate). If you have a husky URDF, hopefully there's another sensor in there you can use as a guide, else, look at turtlebot or other robot configurations for a guide.
Then it will come up every time with your custom URDF.
