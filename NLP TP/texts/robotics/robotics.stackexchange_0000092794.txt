A:

I have updated the Kinetic wiki to reflect the fact that the binary debs are no longer available: http://wiki.ros.org/kinetic/Installation/Debian . You may want to try a newer version of ROS on a newer version of Ubuntu or Debian. Hardware support for very recent graphics cards and CPUs tends to be much better in linux distributions that were created after the hardware was introduced.
