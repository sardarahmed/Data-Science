A:

I solved this problem. In my node I use serial communication with microcontroller so node can't open serial port and crushed.
In general here's what I did that I did:
sudo nano /etc/rc.local

and add
chmod 666 /dev/ttyS1

to change attributes, ttyS1 my serial port
