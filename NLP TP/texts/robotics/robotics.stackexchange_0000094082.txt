A:

ok I found out solution and fixed it. while making the catkin workspace
mkdir -p ~/catkin_ws/src
cd ~/catkin_ws/src
catkin_init_workspace
use the above command to initialize the workspace and then you can just continue with
cd ~/catkin_ws
catkin_make
