A:

There is not a simple way to upgrade to use hydro on Ubuntu 11.10.  hydro is only supported on precise, quantal, and raring (12.04, 12.10, and 13.04)
Target platforms are designated in REP 3: http://www.ros.org/reps/rep-0003.html
