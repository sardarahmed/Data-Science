Q:

revolute and prismatic joint in one

Hello, can i make prismatic and revolute joint in one point( control only for prismatic)?

Originally posted by Pakras on Gazebo Answers with karma: 3 on 2019-07-24
Post score: 0

A:

You can try to model this combination as a revolute joint and a prismatic joint in series with a zero-length link between them.
For example, you would connect your base to the zero-length link using the prismatic joint and then the child link to the zero-length link using the revolute joint.
