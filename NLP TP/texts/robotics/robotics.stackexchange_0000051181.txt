A:

Are you building the project using rosmake or plain make?
The *Config.h file is generated during the dynamic_reconfigure step. BRIDE only generates the .cfg files.
In the CMakeList.txt the triggers to dynamic_reconfigure should be there, it seems they are not called in your case.
