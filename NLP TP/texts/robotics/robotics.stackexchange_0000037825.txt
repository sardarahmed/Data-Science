Q:

how to visualize pcd file.?

Hi all,
i am trying to read .pcd file and view it through pcl_visualizer.
do i have to write separate code for it or can i directly use some commands??
i tried pcd_viewer filename.pcd also, but its not working.

A:

Use pcl_viewer -multiview 1 <pcd_filepath> to visualize the pcd file.
More info is provided on PCL Binaries
