A:

Make sure that your current Boost version has those symbols. I don't remember what commands are used to see the list of exported functions in Linux. On Mac it's otool -TV <library> or nm <library>.
There could be a Boost version mismatch and some functions are missing. I successfully compiled Ogre with boos149. I gave up on trying building gazebo.
