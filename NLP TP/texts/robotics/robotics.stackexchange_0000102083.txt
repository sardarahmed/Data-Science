Q:

[Errno 13] Permission denied: ‘/home/robot/catkin_ws/build’

How can I solve this error?

Originally posted by SRY7 on ROS Answers with karma: 16 on 2022-07-07
Post score: 0

A:

Solved it. Change permission and ownership of the catkin workspace directory:

sudo chown user:user -R /home/user/catkin_ws/devel/
sudo chown user:user -R /home/user/catkin_ws/src/
sudo chown user:user -R /home/user/catkin_ws/build/
