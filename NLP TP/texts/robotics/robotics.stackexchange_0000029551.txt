Q:

Do I need to know any specific computer language or framework to use ROS?

Do I have to know both C++ and Python to use ROS?
Do I need to learn any specific frameworks?

Originally posted by Alex Bravo on ROS Answers with karma: 901 on 2011-02-15
Post score: 1

A:

No.
You can do useful work without knowing either. Both are helpful, but we have successfully introduced ROS to (bright) university freshmen with no previous knowledge of either language.
ROS is the framework. You do need to learn that. But, the introductory tutorials are quite good.
