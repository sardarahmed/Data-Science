Q:

How to increase the noise of odom topics released by gazebo?

I use turbobot3 gazebo to run the SLAM algorithm. I want to evaluate the SLAM algorithm by adding the noise of odom topic. I want to know how to add the noise of odom topic in gazebo9

Originally posted by wugeshenhuaking on Gazebo Answers with karma: 3 on 2022-11-18
Post score: 0

A:

Simply create a node that subscribes to you odometry topic, add noise on your data and republish them to another topic. It's up to you to choose the topics names regarding the rest of your setup.
